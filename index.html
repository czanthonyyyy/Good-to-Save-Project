About Our App

Good to Save is a pioneering project and online platform designed to tackle the critical issues of food waste and hunger in Panama. It connects consumers with surplus food from restaurants and supermarkets at reduced prices, while also providing a means to donate meals to those in need.

Connected with entrepreneurs

We are a project that serves society, being a support for the growing economy in our country, seeking the benefit of entrepreneurs allied to our network, without discriminating size. Micro to large companies are worth equally. As a website, we offer an innovative way for the revitalization of the national economy by reducing losses and increasing profits.

Available to anyone and everyone

Our website has been designed with universal compatibility in mind, ensuring seamless access across all devices and operating systems. Whether you're using a PC, tablet or smartphone, running Windows, MacOS or Android, you'll have full access to our site. We prioritise the user experience by making our website easy to navigate and fully functional on any platform.

Quick and Easy

As well as its development, the website was designed to be easy and fast to access, from the registration process and login, to the navigation through the site. By having an optimized usability, we ensure the comfort and satisfaction of any Good to Save user. Besides that, we maintain a commitment to people with disabilities, keeping all the guidelines to make its use accessible to them.

Anytime available for YOU

Anywhere, anytime. Good to save is always there for you. Our website works every day, including weekends. At the moment, we are working with local restaurants and establishments in the capital, but we plan to expand to all the provinces of the country, following our main goal of expanding to other countries in Latin America.

We protect and care for YOU

We have a strong commitment to the health and safety of our users, customers and sponsors. We ensure that any food or product sold through our platform meets the highest quality standards required by law. We are also committed to keeping all data entered into our systems secure and private. We will never share this information with any third party.

Always a surprise for YOU

To keep you always hooked, in our application you will always find constantly updated offers, as we are based on a daily basis, the surprise box offers are updated daily, providing you total different delicious surprises every day. Remember that we are strongly committed to satisfy you. Allow yourself to be surprised, you never know what new treat you might try!

How Does The App Works?

Explore and Select

Start by visiting the Good to Save website. Browse through our diverse selection of surprise boxes from local restaurants and supermarkets. Each box offers a delightful mix of unsold or surplus food items , giving you a taste of what's insideâwhether it's freshly baked goods, fruits and veggies, or delicious meal portions. Once you find a box that picks your interest, click on it to see more details and add it to your cart.

Order your surprise box

After adding your chosen box to the cart, you'll need to decide how you want to receive it. You can either pick it up yourself or opt for delivery if you'd prefer it to be brought to your doorstep (note that delivery might involve a small extra charge). When you're ready, proceed to checkout to complete your purchase by entering your details and completing the payment process.

Pick Up or Receive Your Order

If you chose pick-up, head to the specified location at the scheduled time to collect your box filled with delicious surprises. If you selected delivery, simply wait for your surprise box to arrive at your desired address. Either way, you're in for a treat!

Enjoy and share your experience

Once you have your surprise box, dive in and enjoy the tasty and affordable food you've purchased. After you've enjoyed your meal, we'd love to hear about your experience! Leave a review on our website to help others know what to expect and to assist us in continously improving our service.

Benefits

From the moment you log in for the first time, you will be able to enjoy and use all the features of our app. However, there is a section for people who wish to help people in need of food and receive certain benefits as they donate or sponsor surprise boxes. To become a sponsor apply at our website, and from that moment you will be able to make your first donation and start receiving benefits for it.
