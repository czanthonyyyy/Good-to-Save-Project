Find answers to common questions about Good to Save, our mission to reduce food waste, and how you can be part of this sustainable initiative.

About the Service

What is Good to Save?

Good to Save is an online platform that connects consumers with restaurants and supermarkets that have surplus food. Our mission is to reduce food waste in Panama while offering quality food at reduced prices.

How does the service work?

The process is simple: establishments post their surplus food at reduced prices, and users can purchase them through our platform. You can choose between picking up your order at the establishment or requesting home delivery.

Where do you operate?

We currently operate in Panama City, but we are in the process of expanding to other provinces in the country, with future plans to expand to other Latin American countries.

Orders and Payments

How can I place an order?

To place an order, simply select the surprise box you want, add it to your cart, choose your delivery method (pickup or delivery), and complete the payment process. You will receive an email confirmation with your order details.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept multiple forms of payment, including credit/debit cards and bank transfers. All our payment methods are secure and protected.

How much does delivery cost?

Delivery cost varies depending on location. The exact price will be shown during the checkout process before confirming your order.

Quality and Security

How do you ensure food quality?

All partner establishments comply with the highest quality standards required by law. We conduct regular verifications and ensure that food is safe for consumption.

What if I have a problem with my order?

If you have any issues with your order, you can contact our customer service immediately. We are committed to resolving any concerns quickly and satisfactorily.

How do you protect my personal data?

Your privacy is important to us. All data entered into our system is secure and encrypted. We do not share your information with third parties without your consent.

For Businesses

How can I join my business to Good to Save?

If you own a restaurant or establishment and wish to join our network, contact us through our business registration form. We will evaluate your application and guide you through the integration process.

What benefits do partner businesses receive?

Partner businesses can reduce their losses from surplus food, attract new customers, contribute to environmental sustainability, and improve their corporate image as a socially responsible company.
